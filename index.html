My Medical Journey

As a 5.2 year medical student in the 6-year program at Tbilisi State Medical University (TSMU), I've developed a strong foundation in clinical medicine through rigorous coursework and hands-on patient care experiences. My passion for medicine stems from a deep fascination with the human cardiovascular system and a desire to make meaningful impacts on patients' lives.

Clinical Interests

I'm particularly drawn to Cardiology because of its perfect blend of physiology, technology, and direct patient care. The heart's rhythmic precision and its central role in human health captivates me. During my clinical rotations, I've discovered a passion for interventional cardiology and electrophysiology, and hope to pursue further training in this dynamic field after graduation.
